Laura Kneale

I am a fourth-year PhD candidate in the Division of Biomedical and Health Informatics in the School of Medicine at the University of Washington (UW BHI) in Seattle, WA.

My research seeks to understand how to optimize healthcare information systems using a socio-technology model of analysis. I am paticularly focused on how information systems can support older adults, their caregivers, and healthcare providers that care for these populations.

I am currently a member of the University of Washington's HEALTH-E lab. HEALTH-E is an interdisciplinary group of researchers committed to exploring systems that support older adults and promote independent aging using innovative technology tools.

I can be found on ResearchGate or LinkedIn , and may be reached at Lkneale at uw.edu.
